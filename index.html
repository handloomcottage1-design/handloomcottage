Care Instructions

How to maintain your Banarasi saree

Cleaning:

  • Dry clean only for silk sarees
  • For cotton/linen, gentle hand wash in cold water
  • Use mild detergent, no bleach

Storage:

  • Store in cotton/muslin bag
  • Avoid plastic packaging
  • Keep away from moisture and direct sunlight

Ironing:

  • Iron on reverse side with cloth
  • Use low to medium heat
  • Do not iron directly on zari work

Our Story

Three generations of Banarasi craftsmanship

Since 1985

Founded in 1985, Handloom Cottage represents three generations of Banarasi weavers preserving ancient Varanasi craftsmanship.

Our Journey

What started as a small family workshop in the narrow lanes of Varanasi has now become a symbol of luxury Banarasi sarees worldwide.

Philosophy

We believe in preserving traditional weaving techniques while adapting to contemporary designs. Each saree tells a story of heritage and artistry.
